Maringá

Introduction

Hello! My name is Iago H. F. de Souza but I go by the artist name Iago Souza. I began playing with fruity loops back in 2008 when I moved to Sweden with my mother. My step-brother introduced me to Fruity Loops and at the time he was learning to produce hip-hop. I started then making my own beats and slowly evolving into making songs with vocals, but nothing too advanced. As the years passed by I started trying out new plugins and making remixes in my free time, trying out different genres such as trance, drum and bass some type of progressive and so on. It was only in the end of 2016 in a trip to my hometown Maringá in Brazil that I got to understand what type of genre I wanted to produce. That's when I first heard Tech in a club.

As a kid my mother and aunt always told me about a club called Nite in the city and that was the hotspot for parties. The club had a lot of history with many djs from all over the country playing there. It was named one of the best clubs in the region because of the quality of sound and the vibe that it had. It felt like you stepped into a spaceship when you were there.

That night, after being at the movies, I convinced my aunt to go there with me to satisfy my curiosity. The line was bending the block, but luckily we found a way to get VIP tickets and skipped the line. The club of the night was called Insomnia with the best djs in town playing. That night completely changed my perspective of how a club should be. That's also the night I got to know my very talented friend Mase (back then he went by Gabriel Andreolli) who has been sharing scenes with big names in Brazil.

When we got back to Sweden, I decided that I wanted to replicate that same genre and vibe that I heard. With a little twist of my own taste. In the beginning it was like learning to walk but slowly I got the hang of it and started making songs with 4-5 mins in length. At the same time I sended demos to Mase and he then asked me if i wanted to make a collab. That got me pretty hyped since it was my first collab. Our first song was Substance. We liked the song so much so we decided to make and ep, the Call EP.

That was my first big release and I was pretty hyped about it. We got some really good feedback for the vibe of the track. That got me really motivated to keep on producing more tracks.

Stepping up

After the success with the Ep we decided to enter a remix competition. That's when Gravity remix came out. It's a deep house vibe with progressive. Pretty nice if you enjoy a kind of mellow beachy vibe.

A while later I got an email about another competition and when I checked it out I got hooked by the vocals and make a remix that's also mellow and easy listening. Unfortunately I didn't win the competition but my music making skills got some improvement.

Meanwhile between the competitions me and Mase produced another collab on a song that I started making.

When I showed the song to my friends and family they got really hyped about it so I got really excited to show it to the world. Mase had some contacts with one of the big deal electronic labels in Brazil so we decided that we would try to release it and after a month or so In the Moment came out.

Upon release we were a bit nervous but soon we started receiving positive coments on the song and tons of likes. This is also a deep progressive house with a little more oumph to it.

The tracks are available to download for free.
